[Intergalactic Avengers]

Contributers: Christine Chee, Amir Fleurizard, Jonothan Sigmon, & Dennis White

-HOW TO PLAY-

In this game you move up and down, side to side and jump from platform to platform. 
You cannot hit the ground or else you will die. You use the mouse pad to aim and click
to shoot at the different types of enemies. This game is set in the setting of
outer space. Enjoy the game!

A - Left
D - Right
W - Up
S - Down
Space - Jump
SHIFT + (A,D,W,S) - Dash
Left Click - Aim and Shoot
R - Restart
